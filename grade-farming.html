Pyramid Head killer grade idea

One of the better structured ways to work on killer grade progress is with a Pyramid Head setup where survivors maintain chase properly and the match is deliberately paced instead of being ended too quickly.

Method summary

Best way mentioned here for killer grades:

  • Use Pyramid Head.
  • Get 5 M2s while survivors maintain chase, commonly around basement structure.
  • Healing side can involve Reactive Healing, Self-Care, Desperate Measures, and Leader on one survivor.
  • Then 2 Corrective Action while they keep chase going during heals.
  • Final down with M2, then cage everyone.

Why this works better than just ending fast

Grade-focused farming is not just about finishing the match. It is about getting the right kind of repeated interaction and pacing so the match gives useful progress instead of being over too quickly.
