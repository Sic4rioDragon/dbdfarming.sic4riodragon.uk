Asian Farming

Easier to get into alone when it works, but a lot less reliable than organized farming.

Hens video Compare with organized farming FAQ Back to home

Why people do it

  • Easy to solo queue.
  • Relaxed when it works.
  • You do not need to round up a full group first.

Main downsides

  • Usually slower.
  • Can go wrong more easily.
  • Can include players who do not know what is happening.
  • Can expose you to griefers or cheaters.

Important note

Asia farming can violate BHVR's Terms of Use due to its unorganized nature and risk of including unwilling participants. It is also unreliable and may expose you to griefers or cheaters.

What is usually expected

Bloody Party Streamers or anniversary cakes are basically required. If you do not bring them, people may crash the server or just shun and kill you.

The rather essential perks are usually Reactive Healing, Self-Care, and Any Means Necessary.

The partner can substitute the Any Means for you, but every missing perk can technically be replaced. It just slows the game down hard and is generally unwanted.

The fourth perk can be basically anything useful: gen progress boost, healing boost, or unhook boost. Usually people take Leader, Prove Thyself, or Deja Vu.

How long does a round take?

A round is usually about 10 minutes and if done correctly you normally max everything, including XP.

Hens' video is more of a showcase of the base idea now. It is a bit outdated, but the core of what people do is still the same. These days everything is generally faster and more optimized.
