What this site is for

This site is here to help explain the main farming styles in DBD and point people toward the actual resources, methods, and communities behind them.

The main thing I want to promote here is organized farming. If everybody in the lobby knows what is going on and is okay with it, that is the best way to do it.

Recommended

Organized farming

Best option if you want the proper way to farm.

  • Everyone knows the plan.
  • Usually done with a full group in VC.
  • Much more efficient than random lobby farming.
  • Best for bloodpoints and currently the best grade method I know too.
Read more about organized farming →

Asian farming

Easier to get into alone, but less reliable.

  • Easy to solo queue into when it works.
  • Usually more relaxed.
  • Slower than organized.
  • Can go wrong or include people who do not want to farm.
Read more about Asian farming →

Bloodpoints & XP

Most people looking into DBD farming mainly want better bloodpoint gain, better use out of offerings, and steady XP while doing it.

A proper round usually takes around 10 minutes and, if done right, you usually max everything including XP.

Grades

Right now the only proper grade method I have here is for killer. For survivor grades, I would recommend Asian farming for now since you usually max everything there.

How do I find my group?

The easiest way is to use the largest and most active farming community out there: the Bloodpoint Farming Discord.

Outside of events it can be a bit more quiet at times, especially between Blood Moon and Anniversary, but people are always welcome to make groups and there is usually at least one farming group around on normal days too.
