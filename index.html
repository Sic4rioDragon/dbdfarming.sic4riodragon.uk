What this site is for

This site is here to point people toward the main farming approaches in DBD and make it easier to understand the difference between them without dumping a wall of text on the homepage.

The biggest one I want to highlight is organized farming. It is usually the cleaner, safer, and more efficient option when everybody knows what is going on and agrees to it.

Main focus

Organized farming

Best option when you can get a proper group together.

  • Everyone knows the plan.
  • Usually done with a full group and voice chat.
  • Much more efficient than random lobby farming.
  • Best fit for bloodpoints, grades, and later questing / achievement runs.
Read more about organized farming →

Asian farming

Easier to try alone, but less reliable.

  • Can be easier to solo queue into.
  • More relaxed when it works.
  • Usually slower.
  • Can go wrong, especially with random or unwilling players.
