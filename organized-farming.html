DBD Farming Guide

Organized Farming

The main kind of farming I want to highlight. It is usually the cleanest and most efficient option when everyone in the lobby knows what is happening and agrees to it.

Join the Discord Open spreadsheet Back to home

Why organized farming is usually better

  • Everyone already knows the goal of the match.
  • You do not have to hope random players understand what is going on.
  • It is much easier to run stronger and more efficient builds.
  • It is better for bloodpoints, better for structure, and usually better for grade-focused setups too.

What you usually need

  • Bloody Party Streamers or anniversary cakes are usually expected.
  • Other bloodpoint offerings can be accepted if everyone is okay with it, but they are generally not popular.
  • Commonly seen perks include Reactive Healing and Self-Care.
  • The exact build depends on the method being used.

How do I find my group?

The easiest way is to join an existing farming community and look for people already running those methods. That is much easier than trying to build a full group from scratch every time.
