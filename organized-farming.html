Organized Farming

This is the main farming style I recommend. If everybody in the lobby agrees to it and knows what is going on, this is the best way to farm.

Join the Discord Open spreadsheet FAQ Back to home

Why organized farming is best

  • Everybody knows the plan before the match.
  • No random unwilling players are involved.
  • It is much more efficient than random lobby farming.
  • It is the best way I know for bloodpoints and currently also the best grade method I know.

What you usually need

  • Bloody Party Streamers or anniversary cakes are generally expected.
  • Other bloodpoint offerings can be accepted if it is communicated first and everyone is fine with it, but they are still usually unwanted.
  • Common perks you will see a lot are Reactive Healing and Self-Care.
  • The exact builds depend on which method the group is running.

How does organized farming work?

All 5 players get into VC, queue together, and follow one of the known methods/builds. That is why it is so much cleaner than Asian farming.

The methods are more efficient, the pace is faster, and if done properly a round is usually around 10 minutes while maxing everything including XP.

How do I find my group?

The easiest way is the Bloodpoint Farming Discord. It is the largest and most active farming community out there.

Right now outside of events it can be a bit more barren, but anyone is welcome to make a farming group and there is usually at least one group online per day on non-event days too.
